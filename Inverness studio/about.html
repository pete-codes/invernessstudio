Peace and quiet

We live on a very quiet road that gets little traffic. The studio is to the rear of the property as well so you can rely on a great night's sleep.

Friendly and knowledgable hosts

We love welcoming people to Inverness and telling them the best places to go. We have lived here for 33 years so we can give you lots of local tips!

Close to the city centre

We are lucky to live 10-15 mins walk to the city centre where you can find pubs, shops and restaurants. We are happy to give suggestions about where to go.
